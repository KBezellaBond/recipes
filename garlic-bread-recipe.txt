Italian-Style Garlic Bread
Cooking Time: 15 - 20 min.
Serves 12-16

1 loaf unsliced French or Italian bread
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 Tbsp parsley, basil, or oregano
1-1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt.

Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Cut bread into 1-in. slices w/out cutting through to 
the bottom of the loaf. Mix the olive oil, parsley, garlic powder, and salt. With a 
pastry brush, spread oil mixture all over, inside and outside the loaf of bread.

Wrap in aluminum foil and bake for 15 - 20 min. until heated through. During last 5
min. of baking, open up foil to crisp.
